About

Welcome to FolkLands!

In this podcast hosted by Tim Downie and Justin Chubb, listeners explore the myths and mysteries of the British Isles, visiting the spookiest and most whimsical locations.

   Tim and Justin are both professional actors and writers who reside in London. Their passion for anything historical and unique truly makes this podcast special. They release an episode of 'FolkLands' every month, since August of 2023.

Justin Chubb is an English writer and actor, best known for his work on 'This is Jinsy', an eccentric award-nominated British comedy series.

Tim Downie is an English writer and actor as well, starring in a variety of media such as 'Paddington', 'Toast of London', and 'Baldur's Gate 3'.
